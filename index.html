    RFID

    RFID technology - RFID stands for Radio-Frequency IDentification, its a small chip which requires contactless. By scanning near the reader the chip can be activated .

    Commonly known for its use in Oyster Cards.

    Contactless Payment

    Contactless Payment - Another form of contactless payment is YOYO wallet. This is used more specificall in University of Hull for Union shops and cafe, enter the card details on app once verified.

    Then scan the barcode on reader, within a few seconds your payment will be paid with a copy of payment history.

    Google Wallet

    Google Wallet - This form of payment is for when you have gift cards the money is then stored on your account under Google Wallet.

    For instance when you redeem a £20 gift, that £20 will be added to your electonic wallet on the app store. So when you buy an app for £5, it will be automatically deducted from the their, rather then your card.

    Apple Payment

    Apple Pay - This form of payment is for when you have gift cards the money is then stored on your account under Apple Pay.

    This is simular to google wallet. For instance when you redeem a £20 gift, that £20 will be added to your electonic wallet on the app store. So when you buy an app for £5, it will be automatically deducted from the their, rather then your card.

    Bitcoin

    Bitcoin - This is a digital payment system where you can purchase bitcoins for real money, then using bitcoin to purchase other products
